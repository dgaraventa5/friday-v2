# Friday Messaging & Positioning

### High-level Positioning

Friday is a task management app that helps you prioritize your daily tasks using proven productivity principles, so you can achieve more with less stress.

### Taglines

Focus on what matters most. 

### Value Props

**Prioritize like a world-class executor**

- **Problem**: Most to-do list apps allow you to enter all your tasks - but they don’t help you prioritize them. Users looking for a solution to get organized often end up with too much information added to their to-do list, leading to cognitive overload and analysis paralysis.
- **Solution**: Rooted in world class prioritization methodologies, our Intelligent Task Prioritization algorithm automatically surfaces your top tasks each day based on importance, urgency, and due dates.
- **Proof points**:
    - **Eisenhower Matrix**: Inspired by the former president ability to ruthlessly prioritize, ‘Urgent’ and ‘Important’ classifications drive clarity for what tasks should be tackled first, and which ones can wait.
    - **Strict daily focus**: Our app’s UI helps you “lock in” on what’s important for making the current day as successful as possible.
    - **Reduce cognitive overload**: You’ll only see that tasks that you have to tackle for the current day in the main view of the app, so all other tasks are “out of sight, out of mind.”

**Organize all of your tasks - all in one place**

- Problem: Tasks across your work and personal life can pile up, creating mental overload that results in you ultimately forgetting to complete to complete them.
- Solution: Rooted in world class prioritization methodologies our app helps track and organize all of these tasks in a easy to follow format. You’re able to create, edit and delete tasks by category. And you’ll be able to customize how much time you spend on each category per day, so that you accomplish your tasks based on your desired work-life balance.
- Proof points:
    - **Category Management**: Balance your life by setting limits for different categories (Work, Home, Health, etc.)
    - **Quick Task Input**: Add, edit, and delete tasks with minimal steps
    - **Recurring Tasks**: Create tasks that repeat on chosen intervals

**A streamlined app to simplify your life**

- Problem: To-do list and productivity apps have an overwhelming amount of tools and features, which can be overwhelming for many users. Getting organized becomes a chore, and the apps end up making users *less* productive.
- Solution: Our app’s clean, simplified UI strips out all complexity of managing your daily tasks. There’s very little manual management of tasks - simply create tasks and let our algorithm do the heavy lifting.
- Proof points:
    - **Clean, Distraction-Free UI**: Focus on what matters with a minimalist interface
    - **Google Authentication**: Secure and easy login
